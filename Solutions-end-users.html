Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a large business to find grants?

Not at all. Many grants are specifically for SMEs, startups, and even sole traders. Our platform helps you filter by business size to find the right fit.

What if no grants are available today?

That's the power of Apex. Grant opportunities are released constantly. If nothing matches today, our 24/7 monitoring ensures you'll get an instant alert the moment a relevant grant becomes available.

Can I apply directly through Apex?

Apex is a discovery tool, not an application portal. We find and summarize the opportunities for you, then provide a direct link to the official source where you can review the full details and apply.

Which projects are typically covered?

A wide range! Funding exists for everything from energy efficiency (solar, insulation), technology adoption (new software, broadband), R&D, community projects, and infrastructure. You set your profile, and we find the matches.
